SWOT Analysis

This web solution automates the process of conducting a S.W.O.T Analysis and doing a S.W.O.T matrix. Users will fill out the survey forms and the system will generate the charts, and other summaries of the results. The S.W.O.T matrix is then displayed from these.
Administrators can now make informed strategic decisions from this matrix.
